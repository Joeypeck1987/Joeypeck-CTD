About

I am learning to turn scattered curiosity into working structures through coding, design, writing, music, art, and the kinds of discipline that survives ordinary days. I like projects that feel half practical and half mythic. Something useful on the surface, but something stranger underneath.

“A page can be plain yet still have atmosphere. A skill can begin clumsy yet still become a craft.”
What I am building toward

A future where creativity and technical skill are not separate lives. I want to understand systems, make useful things, and keep enough artistic nerve to avoid becoming mechanical.
